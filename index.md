# Privacy Policy — Sent

_Last updated: July 14, 2026_

Sent ("the app") is a tool for missionaries and ministry workers to manage the
contacts and partners who support their ministry. This policy explains how your
information is handled.

## The short version

Your ministry data is stored in a private database scoped to your household —
not shared with other Sent users, not sold, and not used for advertising. You
can permanently delete your account and data at any time from Settings.

## What data the app stores

You may enter information such as contact names, email addresses, phone numbers,
mailing addresses, giving/gift records, notes, prayer requests, and tasks, plus
contact photos you add. This information is stored:

- **On your device**, for offline access, and
- **In your household's private database**, hosted on Supabase (a backend
  infrastructure provider), so it can sync across your own devices and with
  anyone you've invited into your household.

A **household** is your private, shared workspace — by default just you, or
you and anyone you've explicitly invited (for example, a spouse or ministry
teammate) using an invite code you generate yourself. Only members you invite
can see a household's data; it is never visible to other Sent users.

Sign-in is handled entirely through **Sign in with Apple** — Sent never sees
or stores your Apple ID password, and can optionally receive your name and
email only if you choose to share them at sign-in.

The developer does not routinely access individual households' data. Limited
access to the underlying database exists only as needed to maintain the
service, investigate a reported problem, or comply with the law.

## Account and data deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time from **Settings → Delete
Account**. If you're the only member of your household, this deletes all of
your household's data along with your account. If you're sharing a household
with someone else, deleting your account removes only your own access — the
shared data remains available to the other member(s). This action cannot be
undone.

## Apple services

The app relies on Apple frameworks that process limited data on your behalf:

- **Sign in with Apple** — handles authentication; see below.
- **MapKit** — when you view the contacts map, addresses you have entered are
  sent to Apple to look up their approximate location, under Apple's privacy
  policy.
- **In-app purchases** — any optional paid upgrade is processed by Apple; the
  developer never receives your payment details.

Your use of these services is also covered by
[Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Infrastructure providers

Sent uses **Supabase** to host its database, authentication, and file storage.
Supabase acts strictly as an infrastructure provider — it does not use your
data for its own purposes. See
[Supabase's Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy) for details on how
they handle infrastructure they host.

## Optional third-party integrations

If you choose to connect an outside service (for example, an email/newsletter
provider), any information you send to that service is governed by that
service's own privacy policy. These integrations are optional and only active if
you set them up.

## Analytics and tracking

The app does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs and does not
track you across other apps or websites.

## Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect
information from them.

## Changes

This policy may be updated as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected
by updating the date above.
